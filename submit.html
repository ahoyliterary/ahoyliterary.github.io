We have two submission periods: one beginning in February for our summer issue, and one beginning in August for our winter issue. Submissions normally stay open for 2-3 months until we have enough submissions. Check our Instagram page for up-to-date information.

If you are thinking of submitting your work, please note the following:

  • Writing must be previously unpublished and written in either English or French.
  • Simultaneous submissions are permitted, but please inform us immediately if a piece under our consideration is accepted elsewhere.
  • Submissions that are sexist, homophobic, transphobic, racist, or generated by artificial intelligence will be rejected immediately.

Poetry: You may submit up to four poems of any length per call. Longer poems may be harder to place.

Fiction and creative nonfiction: For these submissions we have a soft limit of 3000 words; that is, we will not automatically disregard work exceeding this word count, but do understand that longer pieces are generally harder to place.

Art: We accept submissions across mediums including—but not limited to—photography, printmaking, visual research, film stills, photos of installation work. The work will be presented on an even number of pages and should ideally span between four to eight pages. Only one body of work may be submitted per call.

Once you have verified that your submission adheres to the guidelines above, please send it to us using one of the following forms.

  • Ahoy Issue 4 Submission Form (Poetry)
  • Ahoy Issue 4 Submission Form (Fiction/CNF)
  • Ahoy Issue 4 Submission Form (Art)

If you wish to submit both prose and poetry during one submission call, please fill two separate forms. These will be assessed separately; i.e., if you receive a rejection for the poetry piece, this does not mean the prose has been rejected. You may not submit both fiction and creative non-fiction in the same call.
