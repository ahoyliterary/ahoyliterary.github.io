Online orders

We encourage you to support brick-and-mortar stores by buying the latest issue of Ahoy at one of the Montréal bookstores listed above. If the bookstore near you has sold out of Ahoy, you can email ahoyliterary@gmail.com with the subject line AHOY ONLINE ORDER to purchase a copy directly from us. Back issues can also be purchased in this way. Issues are priced at $15.00 and may be picked up in-person in Montréal or shipped anywhere in Canada for an additional shipping fee of $6.50.
