Ahoy is a Montréal/Tiohtià:ke-based literary magazine publishing poetry, short fiction, creative non-fiction, and art. We put out two issues each year: one in the winter and one in the summer.

We are particularly committed to publishing the work of emerging Canadian writers, but we accept submissions from anywhere in the world.

Ahoy Literary Magazine is a federally incorporated non-profit organization.
